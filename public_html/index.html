About The Shop

The Shop is a local neighborhood restaurant located in historic Nob Hill, focusing on made from scratch food that's unique and familiar at the same time. We strive on making the best breakfast and lunch in town and source as much of our ingredients from local purveyors as possible, including produce, coffee, and baked goods. We strive to give you the best experience and food we can produce and give you a comfortable place to enjoy our product and the company of your friends and family.
